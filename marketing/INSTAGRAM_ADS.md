## 📊 Platform-Specific Optimizations

### **Feed Posts:**
- **Image Ratio:** 1:1 (square) for maximum visibility
- **Text Overlay:** Minimal, let visuals speak
- **Colors:** Warm, appetizing, brand-consistent
- **Frequency:** 3-4 posts per week

### **Stories:**
- **Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)
- **Interactive Elements:** Polls, questions, swipe-ups
- **Frequency:** Daily during campaign periods
- **Highlights:** Save best Stories as permanent highlights

### **Reels:**
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds for maximum engagement
- **Music:** Trending audio + original compositions
- **Text:** Large, bold, easy to read on mobile
- **Frequency:** 2-3 per week

### **IGTV:**
- **Length:** 1-3 minutes for deep storytelling
- **Content:** Heritage documentaries, master classes
- **Thumbnails:** Custom designed for brand consistency
- **Frequency:** Weekly feature content
